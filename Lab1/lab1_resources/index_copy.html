What we do:

Code Pink is an international, public facing, non-profit corporation that aims to raise awareness of jelly fish issues and conservation efforts

How you can help:

There's plenty of stuff a motivated individual can do to ameliorate the living condition of jellies globaly, such as:

  • Collecting data on your local jellyfish population
  • Chastizing people who speak ill of jellyfish
  • Providing lodging to traveling jellyfish that chance upon your residence
